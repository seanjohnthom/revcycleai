## Brand Bible

**Target users:** RCM professionals ‚Äî medical billers, coders, practice managers, denial management specialists, AR teams ‚Äî who currently waste hours navigating dense payor portals, outdated PDFs, and hold queues to find coverage rules.

## Compliance & Fact-Checking Protocol

### Before Publishing: Compliance Checklist

Because Axlow operates in healthcare, content must meet higher accuracy standards than typical B2B SaaS.

**‚úÖ Required for all posts:**
1. No patient data, HIPAA violations, or protected health information
2. No medical advice or coverage decisions
3. Factual accuracy on payor policy claims

**‚úÖ Flag for human review if the post includes:**
- Specific payor coverage policies (e.g., "UHC requires prior auth for CPT 99203")
- Policy effective dates or recent changes
- Claims about reimbursement rates
- Testimonials with dollar amounts
- Comparisons to competitors

**‚úÖ Add disclaimers when:**
- Discussing coverage policies: "Coverage varies by plan and state. Always verify with the specific payor."
- Sharing user results: "Results may vary. Individual outcomes depend on practice size, payor mix, and claim volume."

### Fact-Checking SOP

**For posts mentioning specific payor policies:**
1. Verify the policy is current (cross-check Axlow database or payor portal)
2. Include effective date if referencing policy changes
3. Link to source if making coverage claims (in comments, not post body)

**For posts using statistics:**
1. Cite source (internally or in comments if asked)
2. Use realistic ranges (not "saves 99% of your time")

**Red flags that require immediate human escalation:**
- Someone claims Axlow gave incorrect coverage information
- Comments alleging HIPAA violations or data breaches
- Media/press inquiries about Axlow's accuracy

**Scheduling:** Buffer, Hootsuite, or LinkedIn API  
**Analytics:** LinkedIn native analytics + UTM tracking  

**Recommended workflow:**
1. AI generates monthly calendar
2. Human reviews + approves
3. AI generates images via Canva API
4. Human spot-checks images
5. Content loaded into Buffer/Hootsuite
6. AI monitors engagement + replies to routine comments
7. Human reviews weekly performance report

## Human-in-Loop Checkpoints

Even with supervised autonomy, these manual touchpoints are required:

| Checkpoint | Frequency | Time Commitment | Purpose |
|------------|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Initial approval** | One-time | 1 hour | Human approves first 4 posts to ensure brand alignment |
| **Calendar approval** | Monthly | 30 min | Review next month's 16 posts before scheduling |
| **Engagement review** | Weekly | 15 min | Scan comments, reply to flagged issues |
| **Performance deep dive** | Monthly | 30 min | Analyze top/bottom posts, adjust strategy |
| **Quarterly strategy review** | Quarterly | 1 hour | Refresh content pillars, A/B test results, brand evolution |

**Total human time:** ~2-3 hours per month once system is running smoothly.
